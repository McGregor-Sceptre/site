Process

Simple from start to placement

We handle the sourcing, screening, and compliance — you focus on running your business.

01

Tell us what you need

Post your open positions on our employer portal or contact us directly. Tell us the role, start date, location, and number of workers needed.

02

We match & screen

Our team identifies qualified candidates, verifies work history and skills, and prepares them for your review — matched to your specific industry.

03

Review & select

Browse candidate profiles on your dashboard, conduct interviews, and select your team. We handle all H-2B documentation and compliance.
